What we make

Considered work,
built to last a lifetime.

i

Custom Joinery

Cabinetry drawn for a single room and built to fit it exactly. Honest timber, soft-close everything, and lines that sit quietly in the space.

ii

Media Walls

A warm centre for the living room. Concealed storage, integrated lighting and timber that frames the everyday without shouting about it.

iii

Wall Panelling

Fluting, battening and limed finishes that give a flat wall texture and depth. Quiet detail you feel before you notice.

iv

Built-in Storage

Storage shaped to the way you actually live. Hallway seats, study nooks and pantry joinery that make a small home feel generous.

v

Decking

Spotted gum and blackbutt verandahs built for Perth summers. Spaced, oiled and finished to weather slowly and beautifully.

vi

Architectural Timber

Working alongside architects and designers to realise the timber elements that hold a building together, from screens to stairs to ceilings.

vii

Wardrobes

Dressing rooms and walk-in robes with timber interiors, brass detailing and a place for everything. Made to be opened every day.

viii

Custom Doors

Solid timber doors and pivot entries built to weight and hung to last. The first thing a home says, and the last thing you touch on the way out.

The studio

Slow work, by choice.

JT Carpentry WA is a small workshop working with timber across Perth and the western suburbs. Each piece is measured, drawn and built by hand, then finished so the grain stays honest for decades.

We care about the things you stop noticing once you live with them. The way a drawer closes. The line where two timbers meet. The quiet that good joinery brings to a room.

Most of our work comes by word of mouth, from homeowners, architects and builders who want it made properly the first time.
