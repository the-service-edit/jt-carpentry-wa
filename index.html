The studio

We choose the timber first.

JT Carpentry WA is a small Perth workshop. Each piece is measured, drawn and built by hand, then finished so the grain stays honest for decades.

We care about the things you stop noticing once you live with them. The way a drawer closes. The line where two timbers meet. The quiet that good joinery brings to a room.
