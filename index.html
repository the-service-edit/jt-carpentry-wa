About JT Carpentry WA

Built on craft
and trust.

JT Carpentry WA is a Perth-based custom carpentry team delivering quality residential building right across Western Australia. From new homes to fine fit-outs, we bring precision, honest advice and a genuine pride in our work to every project.

We treat every home like it's our own: turning up when we say we will, communicating clearly, and finishing to a standard we'd be happy to live with.
